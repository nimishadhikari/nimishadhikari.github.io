I was born in Kathmandu, in May 1996. For my name my parents used this old religious book, opened it to a random page and picked the name in it. Even though I live in Kathmandu, I am technically not from the city, I am from this small village called Starasaya Phat, right at the base of the Annapurna Mountains! I go there every summer to visit relatives.

I went to Budhanilkantha School for my schooling, and then applied to tufts university and viola! I am here now!!!
